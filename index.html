My Work

Mystic Shores: Family Reunion Adventure

"Mystic Shores" is an intense action-adventure game where players step into the boots of a resilient mariner who, along with his family, becomes tragically separated during a relentless storm. The game unfolds as players guide the mariner through challenging battles at distinct locations. Saving a family member with each victorious encounter, he secures a map that provide clues to locate the others. The game offers a gripping blend of combat, storytelling, and emotional depth as players strive to reunite a fractured family.
